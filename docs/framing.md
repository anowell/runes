# Runes Framing

## Vision

Runes is a developer-first work management system built on simple, file-and-VCS-native workflows. Low ceremony for personal and shared work alike, with high-signal collaboration when it matters.

## Guiding Principles

- **Reduce friction**: fast CLI, minimal required inputs, strong inference. Daily work happens with little thought about tooling.
- **Blazingly fast**: instant feedback. Latency kills the low-friction goal.
- **Embrace developers**: built for the terminal, editors, and version control. Documents are readable, scriptable, portable artifacts on disk.
- **Simple mental models**: projects are folders, records are files, everything is searchable and scriptable.
- **Flexible**: accommodate many workflows, stay extensible. Teams define their layers without confusing the core.

Runes keeps issue history independent from code history while making it easy to link back when needed.

## Future Exploration

- Goal alignment and resource allocation
- Decision lifecycles (lightweight notes through RFC-like decisions)
- Estimation with forecasting and reliability signals
- Advanced insights and reporting

These ideas sit atop the low-friction, fast, flexible foundation.
